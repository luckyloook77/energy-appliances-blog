## Overview
Choosing a refrigerator for a studio apartment means balancing size, efficiency, and features. Energy‑Star certified models guarantee at least 10‑15% less energy consumption than non‑certified counterparts, translating to noticeable savings on electricity bills over the appliance’s lifespan. In this guide we look at five mini‑fridges that are 24 inches wide or less, highlight their key specs, and suggest which might fit different lifestyles and budgets.

## How to Choose
- **Check the Energy Star label** – ensures verified efficiency.
- **Look at annual kWh consumption** – lower numbers mean lower electricity cost.
- **Measure your niche** – allow at least 1‑2 inches of clearance for ventilation.
- **Consider door swing** – reversible doors help fit tight corners.
- **Evaluate smart features** – some models offer app‑based temperature alerts (though rare in mini‑fridge segment).
